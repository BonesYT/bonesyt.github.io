Getting Started

Progress Rise is a game about progress bars increasing. Your main goal is to complete every progress bar.


Go to the 'Progress bars' page to start!

Tips

Click on a button and hold enter! This will make you click it aprox. 30 times per second! You can also click other buttons while holding enter.

Progress Bars

On the progress bars page, click on the circular buttons to increase their value.


Once a bar fills up, its level increases! And it goes back to zero.


You can upgrade the progress bars by pressing the buttons on the right. Do it to increase production.


Press the "Buy next progress bar" button if you have enough points. It will create a new progress bar!


Fact: Increasing the 2nd, 3rd, 4th, etc. bars will increase the speed of the bar above them!

Upgrades

Upgrades are a way to make progress faster.


If you have enough points, you can buy them. They will be useful to make progress.

Sky Layer

Sky layer! Now, instead of increasing when you press the button, it selects the progress bar!


If you have waited points, press the increase all bars button, it'll divide the points into the ammount selected and will give to the progress bars.


ScaleBar

The ScaleBar is a progress bar that increases the 1st layer's speed. Every level, its requirement for the next level multiplies.
